1. Demand Forecasting Models

The Challenge

The company's decision-making was reactive, lacking a forward-looking view of market demand. This led to inefficiencies in inventory and resource planning.

My Solution

I developed and integrated sophisticated time-series forecasting models using PyTorch. These models analyze historical sales data to predict future demand with high accuracy. The entire system was integrated with SQL databases to ensure seamless data storage, retrieval, and automated model retraining, forming the backbone of the company's new data-driven strategy.

2. Comprehensive Financial Forecasting

The Challenge

The finance team needed a clearer, data-backed view of future financial commitments to improve cash flow management and strategic planning.

My Solution

I extended the forecasting system to predict not only sales but also Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) and future purchasing needs. This tool provides a holistic financial overview, enabling more accurate estimation of corporate buying power and enhancing overall financial stability.

3. Intelligent Stock Balancing System

The Challenge

The company faced inefficient inventory distribution, leading to simultaneous stockouts in some locations and overstocking in others.

My Solution

I engineered an intelligent system that leverages the demand forecasts to recommend optimal warehouse transfers. By analyzing inventory levels and predicted demand across the network, this system balances stock, minimizes carrying costs, and prevents missed sales opportunities.
